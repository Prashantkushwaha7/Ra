About Raksha Bandhan

Raksha Bandhan, also known as Rakhi, is a sacred Hindu festival that celebrates the beautiful bond between brothers and sisters. On this auspicious day, sisters tie a sacred thread (rakhi) around their brothers' wrists, symbolizing their love and prayers for their well-being.

In return, brothers promise to protect their sisters throughout their lives. This festival transcends biological relationships and celebrates the universal bond of love, care, and protection.
